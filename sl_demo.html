NCNR Teleoperation Demo

This demonstration show-cases a first implementation of a master-slace tele-operation setup, inspired by typical nuclear sorting and segregation tasks, usually carried out with mechanical teleoperation systems. In this demonstration the slave is a dual arm setup located in the Robotics Lab of the Lincoln Centre for Autonomous Systems (L-CAS), one of the academic partners of the National Centre for Nuclear Robotics (NCNR). The master can be operated here and the kinesthetic control is transferred to the slave side.
